Terms & Conditions

1. Right to Refuse & Refined Pricing

VSR reserves the absolute right to refuse, modify, or terminate any service at our discretion. If a client misrepresents the scope of work or hides existing damage during dispatch, VSR reserves the right to immediately adjust the tier pricing or abort the mission. Misrepresentation of issues voids all pre-paid agreements.

2. Site Safety & Adult Presence

An adult (18+) must be present for the entire duration of any in-home deployment. Pets must be secured. Technicians will not move heavy furniture or work in hazardous/hostile environments. VSR operators will abort any deployment deemed unsafe without refund.

3. Dead on Arrival (DOA) & Diagnostics

If a device is found to be catastrophic or "Dead on Arrival" (DOA) upon technician inspection, the service converts to a Tier 2 Diagnostic ($25 fee). The remainder of higher-tier fees will be refunded. Attempting to conceal liquid or physical damage to secure lower rates immediately voids warranties and forfeits all refunds.

4. Credentials & Software Sovereignty

Clients must have all passwords, PINs, and network keys ready. VSR will not bypass legal password locks. We strictly adhere to digital licensing laws and will not support pirated or unlicensed software.

5. Hardware Procurement & Abandonment

Parts are billed separately and must be paid in full prior to procurement. Any hardware left with VSR for off-site repair must be claimed within 30 days of completion notification or it will be legally deemed abandoned and recycled to cover costs.

6. Pathway Protocol (ECTC Bundle) Verification

The Pathway Protocol ($45) is a subsidized service for active students only. Valid ECTC/KCTCS ID or .edu login verification is mandatory upon arrival. Failure to provide proof reverts the deployment to standard Extended Support ($55) or Premier ($85+) rates.

7. Physical Installation Boundaries

Physical monitor or TV setup is strictly limited to displays up to 50 inches. We do not perform wall-mounting services of any kind.

8. Cancellation & Rescheduling

Notification of cancellation must be provided at least two (2) hours prior to the appointment. Failure to do so forfeits the service fee. Clients are permitted up to two (2) reschedules before the service is permanently canceled.
