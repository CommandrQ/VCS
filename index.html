PRIVACY POLICY

At Vanguard Citizen Services (VCS), your privacy is not just a policy—it is a tactical priority. Our mission is to empower the individual, and that begins with the fortification of your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We only collect information that is strictly necessary for communication and service delivery:

  • Identity Data: Name or alias provided during contact.
  • Contact Data: Email addresses used to reach our support staff and leadership.
  • Usage Data: Technical logs from your browser to ensure our "Command Center" (website) operates correctly on your device.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following operations:

  • Direct communication regarding services and mission updates.
  • Optimization of the Vanguard digital interface.
  • Maintenance of security protocols and prevention of unauthorized access.

3. Data Fortification (Security)

We implement elite-level administrative and technical safeguards. While no digital system is 100% impenetrable, the Vanguard Knights employ strategic measures to protect your information from unauthorized interception or disclosure.

4. Your Sovereignty (Rights)

As a citizen engaged with VCS, you retain full sovereignty over your data. You have the right to request a full report of what data we hold, or the immediate purging of your contact records from our systems.

5. Strategic Alliances

Vanguard Citizen Services does not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Information is only shared if required by law or to protect the safety and integrity of our community.

6. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy or to exercise your data rights, contact the Command Center via the secure comms links on our Team page.
