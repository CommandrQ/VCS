Our Origin

Vanguard Citizen Services was founded by two friends with deep backgrounds in the military and technology world. During our service, we mastered discipline, technical precision, and the duty of care for those around us.

We realized that as digital tools became mandatory for daily life, many in our community were being overwhelmed. We brought our expertise home to ensure no neighbor is left behind in the digital landscape.

Why Vanguard?

A "Vanguard" is the part of an advancing group that scouts the path ahead. In a rapidly changing digital world, we act as the scouts for the Radcliff and Elizabethtown area.

Our mission is to identify threats and build stable foundations so our neighbors can move forward with total confidence. Technology should be a tool for progress, not a source of stress.

The Citizen Code

We don't have customers or clients, we have Citizens. This reflects our core philosophy. Just as the knights of old held a sacred duty to the people they served, we believe our technical knowledge comes with a profound responsibility to our community.

Hardin County Mission

The Radcliff and Elizabethtown corridor is where traditional life meets the digital frontier. Vanguard exists to bridge that gap. Whether securing a small business or helping a senior with their first smartphone, we ensure Hardin County never faces the frontier alone.
