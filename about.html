Our Origin

Vanguard Citizen Services was founded by two friends with deep backgrounds in the military and technology sectors. During our service, we mastered discipline, technical precision, and the duty of care for those around us.

We realized that as digital tools became mandatory for daily life, many in our community were being overwhelmed. We brought our expertise home to ensure no neighbor is left behind.
