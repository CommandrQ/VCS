What is Discord?

Discord is a free app that the Vanguard community uses to talk. It's like a mix between a group chat and a phone call. We use it to coordinate help, share resources, and speak with citizens 1-on-1.

How to Install

  • Windows / Mac Visit discord.com/download and click "Download." Run the file once it finishes to set up the app.
  • Mobile (iPhone or Android) Open your App Store or Google Play Store. Search for "Discord" and tap Install.

Once installed, come back here and click "Join Discord" to enter our Command Center.
