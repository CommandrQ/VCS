Welcome to Vanguard Citizen Services. By accessing our terminal nodes, systems, and networks, you agree to abide by the following operational protocols and privacy standards. Our philosophy is simple: we respect your data, and we expect you to respect the network.

I. Privacy Policy

Data Minimization

Vanguard operates on a strict principle of data minimization. We do not track, scrape, or store unnecessary information. To maintain your Identity Profile, we collect only:

  • Your provided email address (for secure, passwordless authentication).
  • Your chosen full name or callsign.
  • Your Discord ID (if voluntarily provided for communication sync).
  • Your assigned Citizen Rank and role authorizations.

Zero Third-Party Sharing

Your data belongs to you. Vanguard Citizen Services does not and will never sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third-party marketers, corporations, or external entities. Your data remains strictly secured within our encrypted ledger.

No Intrusive Tracking

We do not employ background tracking. We do not log your physical GPS coordinates, hardware MAC addresses, or cross-site browsing history. The network exists solely to facilitate Vanguard operations.

II. Terms of Use

Network Authorization

Access to the Vanguard Command Center and restricted terminal nodes is granted based on verified Citizen Rank. Attempting to bypass security protocols, impersonate Command personnel, or artificially inflate your rank will result in immediate network expulsion.

Operational Conduct

Citizens are expected to act with integrity. You are solely responsible for all actions executed under your Identity Profile. Do not deploy malicious code, disrupt service stability, or transmit harmful materials through Vanguard communication channels.

System Maintenance & Availability

While we strive for 100% network uptime, Command reserves the right to initiate Maintenance Protocols (system lockdowns) at any time to upgrade infrastructure or secure the ledger. During these periods, only authorized Command personnel may access the network.

III. Data Rights & Inquiries

You maintain total sovereignty over your data. If you wish to permanently erase your Identity Profile from the Vanguard Ledger, or if you have inquiries regarding these protocols, please submit a direct request via the Support Terminal.
